About

PolySec lab is a center for education and research in Information assurance, security and forensics at the department of Computer Science of Cal Poly Pomona led by Prof. Mohammad Husain. The lab is to assist the students in developing experience in leadership, development, and administration of Computer Science and Cyber Security academic programs throughout the Computer Science Department. Dr. Husain's academic vision and leadership skills have resulted in state and national-level programs and diverse academic settings across multiple institutions.
